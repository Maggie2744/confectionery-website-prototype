About Us

Coco’s Chocolatiers is one of the oldest and most celebrated chocolate shops in the area. Established in 1894 during an era of industrial revolution, the timeless recipe of its chocolate has never been altered, bringing customers a unique and exquisite taste into history.

Products

We carry a few select products that are still made with the original recipes from 1894. They have lasted the test of time while keeping the heritage alive.
